Acapulco

Scientific Name: Cassia Alata Habitat: Grows throughout all part of the Philippines and blooms in May

Skin
Antibacterial and anti-fungal treatment (tinea infection, ringworms, eczema, scabies, insect bites, skin itchiness)

Menthol

Application

10 minutes

Preparation

  1. Crush leaves and soak in cold water
  2. Twist and squeeze water out and place between sheets of clean cloth
  3. Place on forehead or armpit to lower body temperature
